Pump Power Calculator

Calculate pump shaft power required for centrifugal pumps based on flow rate, head, and efficiency. This calculator is commonly used in pump design and mechanical engineering.

Quick formula

Power (kW) = (Q × H × ρ × g) / (η × 1000)
