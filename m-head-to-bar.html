Meter of Head to Bar Converter

Convert pressure head (meters of liquid column) to bar. This conversion is commonly used in pump engineering, hydraulic systems, and fluid mechanics calculations.

Quick conversion formula

1 meter head ≈ 0.0980665 bar (for water)
